  • once as the form VIII verb ittasaqa (ٱتَّسَقَ)

    • Verb (form I) - to envelops

    (84:17:3) wasaqait envelops وَاللَّيْلِ وَمَا وَسَقَ

    Verb (form VIII) - to be full

    (84:18:3) ittasaqait becomes full وَالْقَمَرِ إِذَا اتَّسَقَ
